Summary

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Valencia, Spain, specializing in high-energy astrophysics, particularly the dynamics of relativistic jets in active galactic nuclei. I have authored several publications and collaborate with international consortia such as H.E.S.S. and Fermi-LAT.
