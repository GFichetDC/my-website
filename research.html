Research

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Valencia in Spain, studying high-energy astrophysical phenomena, with a focus on the dynamics of relativistic jets from active galactic nuclei (AGN).

  • Numerical simulations and modeling of jet-star interactions.
  • Development of computational tools for time-dependent lepto-hadronic modeling.
  • Analysis of data from international consortia (H.E.S.S., Fermi-LAT).

My work aims to reveal the underlying mechanisms of particle acceleration and high-energy emission, including neutrino production in AGN jets.
