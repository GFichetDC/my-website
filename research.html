Research

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Valencia in Spain, where I study high-energy phenomena in the Universe, with a particular focus on the physics of relativistic jets from active galactic nuclei (AGN).

My research includes:

  • Numerical simulations and modeling of jet-star interactions in AGN.
  • Development of new computational tools for time-dependent lepto-hadronic modeling.
  • Data analysis from international consortia such as H.E.S.S. and Fermi-LAT.

I aim to understand the mechanisms behind particle acceleration and high-energy emission, including the production of neutrinos in AGN jets.
